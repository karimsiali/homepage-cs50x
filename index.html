Conquer the lands of your enemies and lay siege to their forts, colonise the new world or simply develop your nation.

you can take what you want

Europa Universalis IV, also known as EU4, is a grand strategy game that starts with real historical borders in 1444, and ends in 1821. The player can pick any nation from that time, and role play as the country itself, build a strong army and conquer other countries. The player can also "play tall", where they don't have to conquer any lands, and instead grow their economy, by developing their states and growing their trade power and economy, in which case they will still need an army in case someone else attacks them. The player can also focus on colonising, either the new world(the Americas) or the old world (Africa and Asia).
